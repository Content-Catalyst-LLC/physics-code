## Reproducibility Goals

A reader should be able to:

1. inspect constants and units
2. compute Schwarzschild radii
3. compute compactness ratios
4. compute gravitational redshift factors
5. compare weak-field and compact-object regimes
6. simulate a pedagogical weak-field precession model
7. inspect metric and curvature metadata
8. compute simple Friedmann expansion scales
9. store assumptions, sources, and simulation runs in SQL
10. extend examples into richer relativity workflows
