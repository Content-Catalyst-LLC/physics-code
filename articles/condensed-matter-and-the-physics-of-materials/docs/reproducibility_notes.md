# Reproducibility Notes

## Data

Primary sample datasets:

- data/transport_sample.csv
- data/material_band_gap_sample.csv
- data/phonon_parameter_sample.csv
- data/materials_property_metadata.csv
- data/defect_metadata_sample.csv

## Reproducibility Goals

A reader should be able to:

1. inspect model transport data
2. compare metal-like and semiconductor-like resistivity trends
3. calculate simple electronic dispersion curves
4. calculate simple phonon-style dispersion curves
5. classify materials by simplified band-gap thresholds
6. store materials metadata, phases, and source notes in SQL
7. extend examples into richer computational materials workflows

## Possible Extensions

Future expansions could include:

- density-of-states calculations
