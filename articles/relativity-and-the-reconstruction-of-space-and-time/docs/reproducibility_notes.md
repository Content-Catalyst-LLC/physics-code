- data/source_metadata.csv

## Reproducibility Goals

A reader should be able to:

1. inspect the constants used in relativity calculations
2. compute Lorentz factors across velocity
3. compare relativistic and Newtonian kinetic-energy scaling
4. transform events between inertial frames
5. verify spacetime interval invariance
6. compose velocities with the relativistic rule
7. compute rapidity and compose boosts
8. compute relativistic Doppler factors
9. store frames, events, constants, and model assumptions in SQL
10. extend the examples into richer special-relativity workflows
