## Reproducibility Goals

A reader should be able to:

1. inspect physical quantities and units
2. check dimensional consistency of core relations
3. propagate uncertainty for derived quantities
4. integrate a simple ODE
5. solve a small eigenvalue problem
6. compute a Fourier spectrum
7. inspect tensor operations
8. build a finite-difference PDE scaffold
9. store equations, variables, methods, and sources in SQL
10. extend examples into richer mathematical-physics workflows
