## Reproducibility Goals

A reader should be able to:

1. inspect common symmetry-to-conservation mappings
2. identify whether a system has time-translation, spatial-translation, or rotational symmetry
3. compute canonical momenta in simple Lagrangian systems
4. verify energy conservation numerically
5. verify angular momentum conservation numerically
6. compute simple symbolic Noether charges
7. inspect field-theory current examples
8. connect quantum generators with transformations
9. store assumptions, systems, symmetries, conserved quantities, and sources in SQL
10. extend examples into richer symmetry-analysis workflows
