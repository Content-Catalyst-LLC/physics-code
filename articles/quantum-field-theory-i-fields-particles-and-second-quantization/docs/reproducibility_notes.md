## Reproducibility Goals

A reader should be able to:

1. inspect constants and unit conventions
2. construct creation and annihilation operators
3. compute number-operator spectra
4. compute harmonic oscillator energies
5. identify truncation artifacts in commutators
6. compute Bose occupation of quantized modes
7. compute scalar-field mode dispersion
8. evaluate simple propagator grids
9. inspect fermionic anticommutation examples
10. store assumptions, fields, operators, relations, sources, and workflows in SQL
11. extend examples into richer QFT workflows
